## 1. Introduction to Quadrant 4

**Quadrant 4** contains all states where the agency dimension is **THEY** (code `00`). These configurations represent business activities driven by **systems, platforms, institutions, and collective entities** — where the agent is not a specific person or team but an abstracted system, market, or institution.

**Key Characteristics of THEY Agency:**
- **Decision-making:** Systemic, rule-based, algorithmic
- **Accountability:** Distributed, institutional
- **Resources:** Large scale, network effects, institutional capital
- **Flexibility:** Low adaptability, high inertia
- **Scale:** Massive potential through network effects

**THEY Code:** `00`
- Bit 1: `0` = collective
- Bit 2: `0` = passive

**THEY Agency Meaning:** Collective entity in a passive/structural role — the system itself acts, not individuals within it.
