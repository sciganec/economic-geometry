## 1. Introduction

Tesla's evolution from a Silicon Valley startup to the world's most valuable automaker provides a fascinating case study in Economic Geometry. Unlike Amazon's relatively linear trajectory, Tesla's path features dramatic pivots, near-death experiences, and multiple reinventions — all while maintaining a consistent long-term vision.

**Timeline Overview:**
- **2003-2008:** Founding, Roadster development, near-collapse
- **2008-2012:** Model S development, IPO, survival
- **2012-2016:** Model S success, Model X, Gigafactory
- **2016-2019:** Model 3 production hell, mass market push
- **2019-present:** Profitability, global expansion, energy business

| Period | Years | Phase |

| 2003-2006 | 3 | SPRING |
| 2006-2008 | 2 | SUMMER (interrupted) |

| 2018-2022 | 4 | SUMMER |
| 2023- | 1+ | AUTUMN transition |

**Total SUMMER:** ~13 years (out of 20)

### 6.2. Multiple Vector Focus

While primarily EAST (technology), Tesla has operated across vectors:

| Vector | Activity | Period |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **EAST** | Core technology, batteries, software | Always |
| **SOUTH** | Direct sales model, service centers | 2012-present |
| **WEST** | Capital raises, stock offerings, Bitcoin | 2010-present |
| **NORTH** | Regulatory credits, emissions compliance | 2009-present |

### 6.3. Agency Consistency

Remarkably, Tesla has remained **WE** agency throughout its history:

| Period | Agency | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| 2003-2004 | WE | Founding team (Eberhard/Tarpenning) |
| 2004-2008 | WE | Musk joins team |
| 2008-2024 | WE | Musk-led team |

**Never transitioned to THEY** — still founder-led, not platformized

### 6.4. The Musk Factor

Tesla's trajectory is inseparable from Elon Musk:

| Role | Impact |
|------|--------|
| Investor | Kept company alive in 2008 |
| CEO | Vision, drive, impossible standards |

**Agency remains WE because Musk is part of WE, not separate from it.**

## 10. Key Takeaways

1. **Vector consistency** — Tesla stayed in EAST (technology) throughout, never pivoting vector
2. **Agency consistency** — Remained WE (team-driven) for 20+ years
3. **Phase volatility** — Dramatic swings from SUMMER to WINTER to SPRING to SUMMER
4. **Near-death recovery** — Survived WINTER state through T_SPRING reinvention
5. **Production hell** — Demonstrated that SUMMER→AUTUMN can be reversed
6. **Peak CES** — 85 in 2021 (higher than canonical peak)
7. **Current phase** — Transitioning to AUTUMN after extended SUMMER

**Final Insight:** Tesla's trajectory demonstrates that consistent vector and agency focus, combined with the ability to survive and recover from WINTER states, can create extraordinary long-term value. The company's refusal to follow the canonical path (staying in EAST instead of moving to SOUTH) has been both a strength (differentiation) and a challenge (production complexity).
